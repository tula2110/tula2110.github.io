Mango Sticky Rice Recipe by TungLam

This is a delicious Thai dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sticky rice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak sticky rice in water for 4-5 hours or overnight.
  2. Steam the rice for 20 minutes until soft and sticky.
  3. Heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a saucepan. Do not boil.
  4. Mix half of the coconut milk with the cooked sticky rice and let it sit for 15 minutes.
  5. Slice mangoes into thin pieces.
  6. Serve sticky rice with mango slices and drizzle with the remaining coconut milk. Garnish with sesame seeds.
